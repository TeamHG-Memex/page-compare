Marvel Heroes 2015 1.45 Patch Notes
Additional Commendation Awards In addition to their current Influence rewards, Genosha Daily Missions now award 4 Hero’s Commendations and 3 Protector’s Commendations. In addition to their current Influence rewards, Genosha Weekly Missions now award 40 Hero’s Commendations and 30 Protector’s Commendations. Influence System Fixes The experience bar and rank icon should now display correctly under the Influence Missions tab. The Weekly Influence cap has been reset for all players, and should now reset correctly each week. The G.L.F. buff has been disabled for this build due to an issue. It may still appear in your buffs panel, but will not give you Influence. In addition, the NPC who gives this buff has also had her overhead icon disabled. A solution is planned for players who missed out on Reputation during the first two weeks of the system. It’s being tested and will be announced shortly. Upgrade Token Boxes Stacking Ultimate Upgrade Token boxes will now stack.
